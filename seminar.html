Seminar information

The CRM-ISM Probability Seminar was founded in 1642 by Jeanne Mance. It is currently organized by Lucas Benigni and Elliot Paquette.

Talks are planned in-person whenever possible.

  • See the calendar entries for the room location.

  • A zoom link will be provided so that the larger seminar community may remotely participate in talks. In the event of scheduling complications, we may also occasionally have talks scheduled only on zoom. Please contact the seminar organizers for access (or use the link provided in the email announcements).
